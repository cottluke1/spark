FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What is your design process?

Our process is collaborative and iterative. We start with discovery and strategy, move to wireframing and design, then to development and testing, and finally, launch and support.

How long does it take to build a website?

A typical website takes 4-8 weeks from start to finish. The timeline can vary depending on the project's complexity and the timeliness of feedback and content from your side.

What's included in the price?

Our pricing includes design, development, basic SEO setup, and deployment. For Custom Sites, domain registration for the first year is also included. Hosting fees are separate.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your website updated, secure, and running smoothly after launch. We handle backups, security scans, and software updates.
